Help

Headcount is an application for the Rochester Institute of Technology's Software Engineering department. It was created by William Leuschner in the spring semester of 2017 to allow lab assistants to record how many people are using the team rooms and the Co-Lab, without having to write down a bunch of stuff on a clipboard.

If you find any bugs, need assistance using the application, or have other comments, send me an e-mail to tech@ and mention "Headcount" in the subject.
